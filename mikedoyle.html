Mike Doyle

Combative and talented footballer at the heart of the great Manchester City side of the 1960s and '70s

Mike Doyle - Captain of the 1976 League Cup Winning Manchester City Team

Here's a quick timeline of Mike Doyle's life:

  • 1946 - Born on 25th November in Ashton-under-Lyne, England
  • 1962 - Joined Manchester City Football Club as a Junior
  • 1965 - Made his senior Manchester City debut playing wing-half
  • 1969 - FA Cup winner with Manchester City
  • 1970 - Legue Cup winner with Manchester City
  • 1970 - UEFA Cup Winners' Cup winner with Manchester City
  • 1976 - Legue Cup winner with Manchester City
  • 2011 - Died on 27th June in Ashton-under-Lyne, England

He was a born winner. He had two periods really as a great player for Manchester City. He was a great leader on the field. I know I got all the praise [for the winning goal against Newcastle United at Wembley in the Football League Cup Final of 1976], but in my mind Mike was our player of the match without question.

Dennis Tueart (former Manchester City and England player)
